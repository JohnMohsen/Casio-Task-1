Casio

Casio Computer Co., Ltd.(カシオ計算機株式会社, Kashio Keisanki Kabushiki-gaisha) is a Japanese multinational electronics manufacturing corporation headquartered in Shibuya, Tokyo, Japan
Its products include calculators, mobile phones, digital cameras, electronic musical instruments, and analogue and digital watches
It was founded in 1946, and in 1957 introduced the first entirely compact electronic calculator
It was an early digital camera innovator, and during the 1980s and 1990s
The company developed numerous affordable home electronic keyboards for musicians along with introducing the first mass-produced digital watches
